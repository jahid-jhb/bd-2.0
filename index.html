Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation in Bangladesh can help the country become a leader in the global knowledge economy. This can be achieved by improving knowledge absorption, creating a culture of innovation, and investing in digital infrastructure.

Sustainability

Sustainability in Bangladesh involves reducing the environmental impact of human activities, while also meeting the needs of the present without compromising the future.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh has been investing in infrastructure to improve its transport and logistics networks. This investment is a key part of the country's economic growth and development.

Empowerment

Bangladesh Peoples Empowerment in Bangladesh has been a focus of development efforts in recent years. Empowerment is a multidimensional process that involves access to resources, education, and employment.
